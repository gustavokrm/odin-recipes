This is a very famous recipe from the state of Minas Gerais in Brazil.

Ingredients

  • 200 g of grinded aged canastra cheese
  • 200 g of sour polvilho
  • 100 ml of milk
  • 40 g of butter
  • 1 egg
  • salt

Preparing

  1. Pre-heat the oven 180ºC
  2. mix the milk and butter on a pan until it boils
  3. pour the boiling mixture on the polvilho and mix until you obtain a paste
  4. mix the remaining ingredients
  5. make small balls with your hands, and distribute them in a recipient and put them on the oven to bake
