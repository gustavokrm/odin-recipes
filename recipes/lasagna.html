4 cheeses Lasagna

How to

First of all, gather all of the ingredients:

  • 3 table spoons of butter
  • 3 cups of milk
  • 100 g of provolone cheese
  • 1 cup of cream
  • 2 table spoons of flour
  • 50 g of gorgonzola cheese
  • 50 g of parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. On a hot pan, mix the butter and flour
  2. Wait until it's not hot anymore, and liquidize it with the remaining ingredients
  3. Put the resulting batter on a pan, to the stove, for thickening
  4. When it's thickened, turn off the heat and mix it so it doesn't coalesce
  5. On the recipient, alternate layers of pasta and batter
  6. Take it to the oven for 10 minutes
